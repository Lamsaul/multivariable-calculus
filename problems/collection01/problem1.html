Problem 1

  1. Evaluate $$\int_0^\infty \frac{e^{-x} - e^{-ax}}{x} \cdot dx$$

Answer

\[\int_1^a e^{-xy} \cdot dy = \left[-\frac{e^{-xy}}{x}\right]_1^a = \frac{e^{-x}-e^{-ax}}{x}\] Thus, for a domain of \(0 \leq x \leq \infty\) and \(1 \leq y \leq a\), \[\int_0^\infty \frac{e^{-x} - e^{-ax}}{x} \cdot dx = \int_0^\infty \int_1^a e^{-xy} \cdot dy \cdot dx = \int_1^a \int_0^\infty e^{-xy} \cdot dx \cdot dy\] \[\int_1^a \left[-\frac{e^{-xy}}{y}\right]_0^\infty \cdot dy = \int_1^a \frac{1}{y} \cdot dy = [\ln{y}]_1^a = \ln{a}\]
