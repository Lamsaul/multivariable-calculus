Problem 9

  1. Show that a constant force field does zero work on a particle that winds uniformly \(w\) times around the ellipse,
    2. \[\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{b^2}=1\]

Solution

\[\vec{F} = p\hat{i} + q\hat{j}\] \[\vec{r} = a\cos{\theta} \hat{i} + b \sin{\theta} \Rightarrow r' = -a\sin{\theta}\hat{i} + b\cos{\theta}\] \[w = \int_C \vec{F} \cdot d\vec{r} = \int_{0}^{2\pi} -ap\sin{\theta}\hat{i} + bq\cos{\theta} \cdot d\theta = 0\]
