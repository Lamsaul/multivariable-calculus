Problem 5

Let \(C\) be the portion of the cylinder \(x^2+ y^2 \leq 1\) lying in the first octant (\(x \geq 0, y \geq 0, z \geq 0\)) and below the plane \(z = 1\). Set up a triple integral in cylindrical coordinates which gives the moment of inertia of \(C\) about the \(z\)-axis; assume the density to be \(\delta = 1\).

Solution

\[\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \int_0^1 \int_0^1 r^3 \cdot dz dr d\theta = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \int_0^1 r^3 \cdot drd\theta = \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{1}{4} \cdot d\theta = \frac{\pi}{8}\]
